Who We Are

Bright eyed bakery was established in 2016 when two old college roommates decided they wanted to make everyones mornings just a little more bearable. From there, they started personally delivering their favorite treats to their favorite people.

Bright Eyed Bakery is a fun, upbeat atmosphere where you can come in and grab your tasty treats to-go, or sit with your pals and catch up over something sweet and delicious. We will warn you, spaces can get crowded so allow some extra time if you're in a hurry!
